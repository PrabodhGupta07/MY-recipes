Taco Mex

Add some pizazz to traditional soft tacos by including corn kernels and black beans in the filling. The mixture also works well in crunchy corn taco shells.

Course: Starter

Cuisine: Spanish

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground sirloin
  • 1 cup frozen whole-kernel corn
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 (15-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 10 (8-inch) fat-free flour tortillas

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat..
  2. Coat pan with cooking spray. Add onion, garlic, and beef; cook 6 minutes or until browned, stirring to crumble beef.
  3. Stir in corn and next 6 ingredients (corn through chiles). Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes.
  4. Warm tortillas according to package directions. Spoon 1/2 cup beef mixture into each tortilla.
